President Xi Jinping has highlighted the issue’s urgency, warning that delayed payments threaten business viability and public trust. Authorities have instructed major banks to offer short-term liquidity loans to help regional governments repay affiliated entities’ debts, though banks are concerned about rising risks and require regulatory assurances.
